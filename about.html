Engineering reliability for industrial teams.

Company Overview

A product and engineering partner for testing, measurement, and automation.

Wucht Engineering specializes in Hantek products, data logger products, engineering equipment, industrial testing and measurement solutions, and industrial automation services. The company brings together product supply, application consultation, and technical support for engineering-led organizations.

Customers rely on Wucht for clear product guidance, timely inquiry response, and solutions that can be scaled across laboratories, manufacturing sites, and industrial service teams.
